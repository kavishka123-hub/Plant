About Our Nursery

Growing Green Since 2010

Verdant Haven began as a small family nursery with a passion for plants and has grown into one of the region's most trusted sources for quality greenery. Our founder, Evelyn Green, started with just a greenhouse and a dream to bring nature into every home.

Today, we maintain those same values of quality, sustainability, and education. All our plants are grown using organic methods, and we prioritize native species that thrive in local conditions.

Our mission is to connect people with nature through plants, providing not just products but the knowledge to help them flourish in your care.
